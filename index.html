Trailhead Screening

This map shows trails and trailheads across Delaware County.

Existing trail segments are shown in green, and proposed/planned/future trails are shown with a dashed black line.

Trailhead color reflects an initial high-level screening, with blue showing lots of opportunity, orange showing some opportunity, and red showing minimal opportunity for on-street connections.

Click on a trailhead to see its prioritization score and the number of stakeholder votes it recevied.

Yellow highlight shows the four areas selected for network planning work, as identified during the most recent project meeting.
